We at Computer Society of India-VIT invite you to attend our free, virtual, hands-on workshop, Bugédex.

Computer bugs can cause security issues, but with the right approach, not only do you get to elevate safety, but stand a chance to be rewarded. Grab the opportunity to interact with cybersecurity experts from CloudSEK and learn about different types of bugs and the threats they pose to systems and organizations, all from scratch.

Bugédex consists of two major parts:

Workshop: An interactive workshop with cybersecurity experts from CloudSEK.

Competition: Report your findings to help the community from your experience, and stand a chance to win amazing prizes.
