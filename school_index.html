We at the CSI-VIT are delighted to announce that we are conducting a free hands-on workshop, BugéDex, in collaboration with experts from CloudSEK. CSI-VIT is a non-profit technical chapter of VIT, Vellore that aims towards an easily accessible platform for the learning community to gain knowledge and showcase skills through workshops, hackathons and technical events.

In the digital era we live in, protection of data from security breaches is a must as malicious attacks continue to be a threat to global organisations and their products. Thus, to bring awareness about this matter to the masses, CSI presents BugéDex, a workshop where anyone can dive into cybersecurity and get the best possible start.

The workshop will provide a crash course and a hands-on experience to the attendees on bug bounty and technical reporting. Participants will learn the following in the workshop:
Introduction to bugs/errors
How to find Functional bugs, Security bugs and Design bugs
Reporting - A guide on how to write a bug bounty report

Blog writing:
The task is to write a blog on the attendee's takeaways from the workshop, and help the community learn from their experience. The better the learnings and illustrations of the workshop takeaways, the higher the chances to win prizes!
Prizes include: iPad 9th Gen (Worth 30k), OnePlus Watch (Worth 15k) Google Pixel Buds (Worth 10k), Amazon Echo Dot (Worth 5k), Mi Band 6 (Worth 3.5k), 5 Boat Headphones (Worth 2k each).

We believe this workshop will undoubtedly help students of your school to get a strong foundation in the very critical and lucrative field of cyber security. With the amazing prizes and the unique chance to interact with the brightest minds from the industry, it will benefit all attendees.
